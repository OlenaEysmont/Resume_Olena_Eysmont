    • Wireframes Creation

    The term "Wireframe Creation" refers to the development of the skeletal structure of an interface, typically representing a simple, low-fidelity layout of a webpage or application.

    This design phase aims to define the structure and arrangement of essential elements on the page without considering design details.

    Page Structure

    Wireframes help determine where various elements such as headers, text, images, buttons, and other components will be placed.

    Content Distribution

    They show how content will be organized on the page to provide a logical and user-friendly interaction.

    Functionality

    They assist in defining functional elements like forms, buttons, links, and other interactive components.

    Navigation

    TWireframes outline how users will navigate the site or application, indicating different sections and transitions.
